Moscow mule

Description

The best cocktail ever!

Ingredients

  • ice
  • Â½ fresh lime
  • 2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 4 fluid ounces ginger beer, or to taste
  • 2 lime slices

Steps

  1. Fill a tall glass with ice.
  2. Squeeze 1/2 lime over ice.
  3. Pour vodka over ice and top with ginger beer.
  4. Garnish with lime slices.
